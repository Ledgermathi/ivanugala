The Origin

Started by one guy. Became a family.

L
Mathimukilan — "Ledger" Founder · lowkey · gamer

Ivanugala was formed by Mathimukilan — the homies just call him Ledger. He's lowkey about it, but everyone knows the crew doesn't exist without him.

Originating from ECE‑A, Batch 2025–2029, the team is known for being unpredictable, chaotic, and naturally funny. Every moment they spend together turns into a memory worth remembering.

Ivanugala is more than just a group of classmates or teammates — they are brothers. They stand by one another during the toughest times, celebrate every victory together, and never leave a member behind.

No ego. No jealousy. All loyalty.

What truly sets Ivanugala apart is their bond — built on trust, loyalty, respect, and genuine friendship. They work on projects together, learn together, grow together, and create wholesome memories that last a lifetime. Every member wants to see the others succeed, believing that one person's success is a victory for the entire brotherhood.
